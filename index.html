My lab's research focuses on combining engineering dynamics principles with advanced signal processing techniques to leverage wearable sensor technologies like inertial measurement units (IMUs) and surface electromyography (sEMG) to better understand human performance, health, and behavior. The future of my lab will continue these lines of research in addition to utilizing these methods to better understand human-robot interactions.
