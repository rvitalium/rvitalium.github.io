I am an Assistant Professor in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Iowa.Prior to joining the department, I was a NASA-funded TRISH postdoctoral fellow at the University of Michigan, where I also obtained my Bachelors, Masters, and Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering.

My research focuses on combining engineering dynamics principles with advanced signal processing to capitalize on the advantages of using wearable sensor technologies like inertial measurement units (IMUs) and surface electromyography (sEMG) to better understand human performance, health, and behavior. The future of my lab will continue these lines of research in addition to using these methods to better understand human-robotic interactions.
