Dr. Rachel Vitali is a graduate of the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor, where she completed her undergraduate and graduate studies. Prior to her appointment at the University of Iowa, she was a NASA-funded TRISH postdoctoral researcher in the Industrial and Operations Engineering Department at the University of Michigan. Her research interests include advancing the use of wearable sensor technologies to evaluate human performance, health, and behavior in natural environments as well as applications for human-robotic interactions.
